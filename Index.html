Frequently Asked Questions

What courses are offered at ACN College?

We offer undergraduate and postgraduate courses including B.Tech (in various engineering streams), MBA, Diploma (Polytechnic), BBA, and BCA.

How can I apply for admission?

You can apply online through our official website or by visiting the campus admissions office. For B.Tech, admissions may be based on UPSEE/JEE scores, and for MBA, CAT/MAT scores are considered.

Does the college provide placement support?

Yes, our dedicated Training & Placement Cell works tirelessly to assist students with internships and final job placements through campus drives and strong industry tie-ups.
